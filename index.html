  • What types of documents can I interact with?
    Our application supports a wide range of document formats, including PDF, Word (.docx), and scanned PDF documents. For scanned PDFs, we use Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract text.
  • How does pAIpyrus work?
    Simply upload your document, ask any questions related to the content, and the application will retrieve the relevant information from the document.
  • What is OCR, and how does it help with scanned documents?
    Our AI copywriting tool uses sophisticated algorithms to understand context, tone, and language nuances. Users input specific details or prompts, and the AI generates high-quality content based on that input, refining.

    No, you cannot
  • Is there a limit to the size or number of documents I can upload?
    No, but we recommend that you do not upload too many files.
  • Can I ask multiple questions about the same document?
    Yes, you can
